Often reported as "black preview", "no preview showing", "right side/large preview blank", "unable to see eye", or similar descriptions by customers. The underlying issue is the infrared preview display on the Acquire window is not able to display a clear image.

In this image, the area labeled #8 indicates the infrared preview display. When the customer is experiencing this issue, this large preview area will appear dark. Note that in most instances of this issue, the output from the image capture is normal, although customers will likely complain that it is difficult to get a quality image without a preview image.
