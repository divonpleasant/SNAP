Cirrus Photo Reference Index

Description

Cirrus Photo (CP) is a combination Visucam and OCT scanning device that takes quality fundus images combined with quality OCT scans.

The camera is "non-mydriatic" meaning, in most cases, pupil dilation is not required to get quality color images of the fundus. However, Fluorescein Angiography (FA) will always require pupil dilation.
