VisuCam Reference Index

Description

VisuCam is a fundus camera used to take photographs of the internal eye (fundus) and external eye structures.

The VisuCam is "non-mydriatic" meaning, in most cases, pupil dilation is not required to get quality color images of the fundus. However, Fluorescein Angiography (FA) will always require pupil dilation.
