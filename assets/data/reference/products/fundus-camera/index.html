Fundus Camera Reference Index

Description

Zeiss Fundus Cameras (such as the FF450+) are dumb boxes of lenses and filters. There is no PC associated if there are camera-specific hardware issues.

A VisuPac PC can drive the FF450+ camera. If it is a Visupac/FF450+ system, there will also be Zeiss digital sensors attached to capture images (ZK-5/Dolphin).

The FF450+ can also be driven by 3rd party applications w/PC and digital sensors. If there is a problem with an FF450+ connected to a 3rd party system and the camera is suspect, the FSE can check the camera only and should not touch the 3rd party hardwar and/or software. The main 3rd party vendor will have a blue digital sensor attached to the top of the FF450+.

For VisuPac PC or application issues, always get the VisuPac's serial number. Fundus camera hardware and VisuPac PCs have separate serial numbers in CRM.
